460 Deaths and 243,612 Injuries from COVID-19 Jabs Reported in the U.K. 
HAFMarch 7, 2021 
  
by Brian Shilhavy
The UK Government’s reporting system for COVID jjab adverse reactions from the Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency released their latest report today, March 4, 2021.

The report covers data collected from December 9, 2020, through February 21, 2021, for the two experimental COVID jjabs currently in use in the U.K. from Pfizer and AstraZeneca.
They report a total of 460 deaths and 243,612 injuries.
