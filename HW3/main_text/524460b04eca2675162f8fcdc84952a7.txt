Frequently Asked Questions



How can I pay for my membership?






You can choose to pay using a debit or credit card, or by Paypal. Monthly memberships can be cancelled at any time.
We are sorry, but currently we cannot accept cheque payments.







How do I access members only content?






To access members-only content, such as UK Column News Extra, you need to be a current member and be logged in.







How do I change my password?






To change your password, click on the Log In link in the top menu.
The login box will appear.
Click on Forgotten Password?
You will receive an email with a one time link. Click on that link.
You will be taken to the 'Reset Password' page. Click on 'Log In'.
Enter your new password in the Password and Confirm Password boxes, and click Save.
That’s it.
If you haven’t kept your email address up to date, it will not be possible to change your password this way. Please use this form to contact us, and select 'Membership Issues' as the category so that it goes to the correct person.
