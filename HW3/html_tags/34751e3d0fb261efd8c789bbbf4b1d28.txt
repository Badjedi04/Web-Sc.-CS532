<h2><strong>Introduction</strong></h2>
<p><i>There is a sequence of outright lies and fabrications used to justify far-reaching policy decisions which in the course of the last 18 months are literally destroying people’s lives Worldwide. </i></p>
<p><i>“Fake science” is used to </i><i>justify confinement, social distancing, the face mask, the prohibition of social gatherings,  cultural and sports events, the closure of economic activity, all of which are upheld as a means to repealing the “killer virus”. </i></p>
<p><i>Who is this “Killer Virus” which has been personified by both the media and our governments, held responsible for triggering economic and social chaos Worldwide? </i></p>
<p><i>You might recall that a</i><i>t the height of the February 2020 financial collapse, “V the Virus” was held responsible for the largest stock market crash since 1929. </i></p>
<p><strong><em> Has the “Killer Virus” been Identified. Has SARS-CoV-2 been Isolated?</em></strong></p>
<p><i>This article will review this contentious issue starting at the outset of the crisis in January 2020. Part of this analysis is based on research conducted in early 2020. </i></p>
<p><i>The central question raised in this review is the following: is there reliable evidence provided by the WHO and national  health authorities that the alleged <strong>SARS-CoV-2</strong>  virus has been isolated/purified  from an “unadulterated sample taken from a diseased patient”? </i></p>
<p><i>While the alleged virus was initially defined as the</i><strong style="font-style: italic;"> 2019 novel coronavirus (2019-nCoV) </strong><i>in January 2020, <a href="https://www.eurosurveillance.org/content/10.2807/1560-7917.ES.2020.25.3.2000045">the World Health Organization (WHO) did not have in its possession</a> details regarding the isolation/purification and identity of  <strong>2019-nCoV.</strong></i></p>
<p><i>And because details concerning <strong>isolation / purification</strong> were not available, the WHO decided to “customize</i><i>” The Real Time Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (rRT-PCR) Test </i><i>using the alleged “similar”<strong> 2003 SARS virus</strong> (subsequently renamed SARS-1) as “a point of reference” for detecting genetic fragments of the <strong>novel 2019-nCoV. </strong></i></p>
<p><i>What this decision entails is that novel 2019-CoV-2 is NOT a <strong>novel virus</strong>. It was categorized by the Chinese authorities and the WHO as “similar” to the<strong> 2003 SARS-CoV</strong> as well as to <strong>MERS.</strong> </i></p>
<p><i><strong>2003 SARS-CoV</strong> was subsequently renamed <strong>SARS-CoV-1.</strong></i></p>
<h2><i></i><strong>History: Isolation of the Virus </strong></h2>
<p><strong>Chinese Health Authorities</strong></p>
<p><i>The Chinese authorities announced on January 7, 2020 that </i><strong style="font-style: italic;">“a new type of virus”</strong><i>  had been identified  “</i><a href="https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC7159299/" style="font-style: italic;">similar to the one associated with SARS and MERS</a><i>” (related report , not original Chinese government source).  </i><i>The underlying method is described below:</i></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><em>We prospectively collected and analysed data on patients <strong>with laboratory-confirmed 2019-nCoV infection by real-time RT-PCR and next-generation sequencing. </strong></em></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><em>Data were obtained with standardised data collection forms shared by WHO and the International Severe Acute Respiratory and Emerging Infection Consortium from electronic medical records. (emphasis added)</em></p>
<p>The  following article entitled <a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2008-3">“</a><b><a href="https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2008-3">A new coronavirus associated with human respiratory disease in China</a>, </b>(Nature, February 3, 2021) was among the first to report on China’s novel coronavirus:</p>
<div style="padding-left: 30px;"><i>…[We] collected<strong> bronchoalveolar lavage fluid (BALF) </strong>and performed deep meta-transcriptomic sequencing. The clinical specimen was handled in a biosafety level 3 laboratory at Shanghai Public Health Clinical Center. <b>Total RNA was extracted from 200 μl of BALF</b> and a meta-transcriptomic library was constructed for pair-end (150-bp reads) sequencing using an Illumina MiniSeq as previously described 4,6,7,8.</i></div>
<div style="padding-left: 30px;"> .</div>
<div style="padding-left: 30px;"><i>In total, <b>we generated 56,565,928 sequence reads that were de novo-assembled </b>and screened for potential aetiological agents. </i></div>
<div style="padding-left: 30px;">…</div>
<div style="padding-left: 30px;">.</div>

<p>SARS-CoV-2 has not been isolated. Does the virus Exist?</p>
<p>Neither the Chinese authorities nor the CDC, the WHO, national governments, scientific /  health authorities have provided evidence that SARS-CoV-2 has been  isolated /purified.</p>
<p>Based on the investigative research of <strong>Christine Massey</strong> we have access to the responses of numerous governments and health authorities, including that provided by the Republic of Ireland to journalist <strong>Gemma O’Doherty.</strong></p>
<p>What this means is that the entire covid narrative falls flat.</p>
<p>We have been systematically misled.</p>
<p>Everything you have been told by your governments is a lie, a complexity of lies and falsehoods.</p>
<p>There is no pandemic. The isolation / purification of the virus has not been undertaken.</p>
<p>All the policies adopted by governments worldwide allegedly to “save lives” are illegal, socially destructive and in violation of fundamental human rights.</p>
<p><strong>These policies have been instrumental in “destroying people’s lives”.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Dr. Stephen Frost  </strong>refers to the alleged “Covid pandemic” as<strong> </strong>“<strong>The Biggest Medical Fraud in World History”.</strong></p>
<p>From the outset in January 2020, the flawed and invalid RT-PCR test was used to “detect” the alleged 2019 SARS-CoV-2 virus,  despite the fact that details regarding the isolation/purification of the original virus were not available.</p>
<p>All far-reaching policy decisions imposed on people Worlwide were based on a data bank of fake <a href="https://www.globalresearch.ca/the-2020-worldwide-corona-crisis-destroying-civil-society-engineered-economic-depression-global-coup-detat-and-the-great-reset/5730652"> case positives coupled with false mortality data </a>pertaining to Covid-19 related deaths.</p>
<p>Curbing the alleged SARS-CoV-2 pandemic through the imposition of face masks, social distancing, closing down of national economies has absolutely no validity,</p>
<p>The original strain of SARS-CoV-2 has not be isolated /purified: How does that affect the process of so-called “detection” of the “deadly variants” of the original virus?</p>
<p><strong>Mortality and Morbidity: While there is “No Killer Virus”, there is a “Killer Vaccine”.</strong></p>
<p>While the SARS-CoV-2 virus is presented by the media and the governments as a “killer virus” (when in fact the WHO and CDC describe it as “similar to seasonal influenza”), a totally invalid and dysfunctional <strong>Covid -19 vaccine is currently being imposed on the entire population of Planet Earth: 7.9 billion people.</strong></p>
<p>It’s a multibillion dollar endeavour with Pfizer in the lead, establishing a near Worldwide monopoly for the sale and distribution of the mRNA killer vaccine.</p>
<p><strong>Important Question: </strong></p>
<p>How did Big Pharma manage to develop a vaccine (sponsored by the WHO, GAVI, the Gates Foundation, et al) with a mandate “to protect people” against a virus which has not been isolated/ purified  from an “unadulterated sample taken from a diseased patient”?</p>
<p>Vaccine in relation to What? The virus has not been identified.</p>
<p>Moreover, <strong>2019</strong> <strong>SARS-CoV-2</strong> has been categorized as similar to the <strong>2003 SARS-CoV</strong> which means that the <strong>2019 SARS-CoV-2 is not a novel virus. </strong></p>
<p>The legitimacy of the Covid vaccine project hinges upon the validity of hundreds of thousands of RT-PCR fake positive cases Worldwide combined with fake Covid related mortality data.</p>
<p><strong>Big Pharma’s mRNA vaccine has resulted in countless deaths and injuries Worldwide which are barely reported by the mainstream media. </strong></p>
<p>While we do not have figures for the entire Planet, the latest official figures for the European Union and the U.S are revealing. Bear in mind they vastly underestimate the real trends in vaccin related mortality and morbidity:</p>
<div style="padding-left: 30px;">EU/EEA/Switzerland to 31 July 2021 – 20,595 Covid-19 injection related deaths and over 1.94 million injuries, per EudraVigilance Database.</div>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">UK to 21 July 2021 – 1,517 Covid-19 injection related deaths and over 1.1 million injuries, per MHRA Yellow Card Scheme.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">USA to 23 July 2021 – 11,940 Covid-19 injection related deaths and over 2.4 million injuries, per VAERS database.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>TOTAL for EU/UK/USA – 34,052 Covid-19 injection related deaths and over 5.46 million injuries reported as at 1 August 2021</strong></p>

Furthermore, people continue to die (and suffer injury) from the injections with every day which passes.  (<a href="https://www.globalresearch.ca/the-killer-vaccine-worldwide-7-9-billion-people/5749363">D4CE</a></p>
