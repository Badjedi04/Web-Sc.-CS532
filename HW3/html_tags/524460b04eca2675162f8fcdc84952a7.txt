How do I change my password?
</button>
</div>
<div class="accordion-pane__content ui-accordion-content ui-helper-reset ui-widget-content ui-corner-bottom" style="display:none">
<div class="field field--name-field-paragraphs">

<div class="inner">
<div class="field field--name-field-text"><p>To change your password, click on the Log In link in the top menu.</p>
<p>The login box will appear.</p>
<p>Click on Forgotten Password?</p>
<p>You will receive an email with a one time link. Click on that link.</p>
<p>You will be taken to the 'Reset Password' page. Click on 'Log In'.</p>
<p>Enter your new password in the Password and Confirm Password boxes, and click Save.</p>
<p>That’s it.</p>
<p>If you haven’t kept your email address up to date, it will not be possible to change your password this way. Please <a href="https://www.ukcolumn.org/contact">use this form to contact us</a>, and select 'Membership Issues' as the category so that it goes to the correct person.</p>
