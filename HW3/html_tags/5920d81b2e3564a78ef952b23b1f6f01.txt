<p>As can be expected when new experimental “vaccines” that are not approved by the FDA are given emergency use authorization to fight a “pandemic” that is now over a year old, reported deaths following the injections of these shots have now skyrocketed in the U.S. population by over 6000% here at the end of the first quarter of 2021, as compared to recorded deaths following FDA-approved vaccines at the end of the first quarter of 2020.</p>
<p>These new products, which many doctors and scientists claim <a href="/v3/r2/?url=aHR0cHM6Ly9oZWFsdGhpbXBhY3RuZXdzLmNvbS8yMDIxL2RyLWRhdmlkLW1hcnRpbi1vbi1leHBlcmltZW50YWwtbXJuYS1jb3ZpZC12YWNjaW5lcy10aGlzLWlzLW5vdC1hLXZhY2NpbmUtaXQtaXMtYS1tZWRpY2FsLWRldmljZS8=" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">do not even meet the legal definition of a “vaccine</a>,” are described by the manufacturers themselves as “<a href="/v3/r2/?url=aHR0cHM6Ly9oZWFsdGhpbXBhY3RuZXdzLmNvbS8yMDIxL3RoZS1uZXctbXJuYS1jb3ZpZC12YWNjaW5lcy1pbmplY3QtYW4tb3BlcmF0aW5nLXN5c3RlbS1pbnRvLXlvdXItYm9keS1ub3QtYS1jb25zcGlyYWN5LXRoZW9yeS1tb2Rlcm5hLWFkbWl0cy1pdC8=" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">operating systems</a>” called the “<a href="/v3/r2/?url=aHR0cHM6Ly9oZWFsdGhpbXBhY3RuZXdzLmNvbS8yMDIxL21vZGVybmFzLXRvcC1zY2llbnRpc3Qtb24tbXJuYS10ZWNobm9sb2d5LWluLWNvdmlkLXNob3RzLXdlLWFyZS1hY3R1YWxseS1oYWNraW5nLXRoZS1zb2Z0d2FyZS1vZi1saWZlLw==" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">software of life</a>,” and prior to COVID they have never been approved to be used on human populations.</p>
<p>There are literally thousands of doctors and scientists around the world who have spoken out against these experimental injections, some even calling them “<a href="/v3/r2/?url=aHR0cHM6Ly9oZWFsdGhpbXBhY3RuZXdzLmNvbS8yMDIwL2NvdmlkLXZhY2NpbmVzLWJpb2xvZ2ljYWwtd2VhcG9ucy1vZi1tYXNzLWRlc3RydWN0aW9uLXNheXMtd3lvbWluZy1tZWRpY2FsLWRvY3Rvci1hbmQtbWFuYWdlci1mb3Itd3lvbWluZ3Mtc3RhdGUtcHVibGljLWhlYWx0aC1kZXBhcnRtZW50Lw==" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">biological weapons of mass destruction</a>.”</p>
<p>Their voices are censored in the pharma-controlled corporate media and by Big Tech, so the people dying and becoming injured by these injections are the pro-vaccine people who primarily only get their information from these censored sources that are funded by Wall Street corporate billionaires, such as Bill Gates.</p> <p>

<p>Since today, the day this report is being written and published, is the last day of March, 2021, we do not have complete stats from VAERS on injuries and deaths following vaccination yet. But here’s what we know based on what the CDC has published through yesterday, March 30, 2021.</p>
<p>The last data dump into VAERS was published last week on March 26, 2021, and it listed 2050 deaths following the experimental COVID injections. See the <a href="/v3/r2/?url=aHR0cHM6Ly9oZWFsdGhpbXBhY3RuZXdzLmNvbS8yMDIxL2NkYy0yMDUwLWRlYWQtZm9sbG93aW5nLWNvdmlkLXZhY2NpbmVzLWFzLTMwMC1kZWF0aHMtYWRkZWQtdGhpcy13ZWVrLTE2LWRlYXRocy1mcm9tLW5ldy1qai1zaG90LXByb2R1Y2luZy1jb3ZpZC1zeW1wdG9tcy8=" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">report here</a>.</p>
<p>However, some of those deaths following the COVID injections occurred in December, 2020, when the Pfizer and Moderna shots were issued EUA’s by the FDA.</p>
<p>So we ran the report for this year, 2021, from which we know the data is only current through March 19, 2021, and it showed 1,754 deaths following ALL vaccines, not just the COVID injections.</p> <p>

<p>How is this NOT a national tragedy that should be headline news everywhere??</p>

<p>Yesterday, the CDC reported that deaths following COVID injections are now 2,509. (<a href="/v3/r2/?url=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuY2RjLmdvdi9jb3JvbmF2aXJ1cy8yMDE5LW5jb3YvdmFjY2luZXMvc2FmZXR5L2FkdmVyc2UtZXZlbnRzLmh0bWw=" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">Source</a>.)</p>

So 1754 plus these 459 deaths gives us the total deaths so far through March 2021, which is 2,213, although after the next data dump in to VAERS this coming Friday, this number will increase even more when we add the non-COVID vaccine deaths also.</p><p><div class="rotating_text_link" id="rotating_text_link_24"></div></p>
<p>That is an increase of over 6000% from last year during the same time period.</p>
<p>The increase in deaths reported is most certainly related to the new experimental COVID injections, and yet the CDC and FDA’s position is that NOT ONE of these deaths are related to the COVID injections.</p>
