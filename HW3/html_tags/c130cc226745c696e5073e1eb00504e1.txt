<h2><strong>Introduction</strong></h2>
<p><i>Is a new worldwide lockdown envisaged as a means to combating the “dangerous” Covid variant entitled </i><strong><em>“Delta”?</em></strong></p>
<p><i>The fear campaign has once more gone into high gear.</i></p>
<p><i>Let me briefly review the history of this crisis. </i></p>
<p><i>There is a sequence of outright lies and fabrications used to justify far-reaching policy decisions in the course of the last 18 months. </i></p>
<p><i>The biggest lie, which is firmly acknowledged both by scientific opinion and the WHO is that the <strong>RT-PCR test used to “detect” the spread of the virus (as well as the variants) is not only flawed but TOTALLY INVALID. </strong>(<a href="https://www.globalresearch.ca/the-2020-worldwide-corona-crisis-destroying-civil-society-engineered-economic-depression-global-coup-detat-and-the-great-reset/5730652">Michel Chossudovsky, Chapter III</a>)</i></p>
<p><i>From the outset in January 2020, all far-reaching policy decisions upheld and presented to the public as a “means to saving lives” were based on  <a href="https://www.globalresearch.ca/the-2020-worldwide-corona-crisis-destroying-civil-society-engineered-economic-depression-global-coup-detat-and-the-great-reset/5730652">flawed and invalid RT-PCR case positives coupled with false mortality data </a>pertaining to Covid-19 related deaths: Tn the US “The underlying Cause of death” is Covid-19 <strong>“more often or not”</strong> (CDC). No autopsy required.</i></p>
<p><i>These Covid “estimates” were used to justify confinement, social distancing, the face mask, the prohibition of social gatherings, cultural and sports events, the closure of economic activity. </i></p>
<p>The crisis was marked by several important phases:</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>1. Crisis in Air Travel and International Transport</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>The calling of a public health emergency of international concern (PHEIC)</strong> by the WHO Director General on January 30, 2020 was instrumental in launching the coronal crisis. There were <strong>83 positive cases</strong> outside China out of a population of 6.4 billion.<strong> There was no emergency</strong>:  Ironically, the flawed and invalid RT-PCR test was used to estimate those 83 positive cases.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">On the following day president Trump’s ordered the closing down of air travel with China which marked <strong>the onset of a crisis in International Air Travel and Transport </strong>which has extended its grip over a period of 18 months leading to the bankruptcy of airline companies Worldwide, the destruction of the tourist industry, a major crisis in commodity trade, etc.  This was a deliberate act to précipitate the demise of Air Travel Worldwide. There were 5 positive cases in the US, which were used to justify Trump’s decision on  January 31st, 2020.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">2. <strong>The  February Financial Crash on February 20, 2020</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">It was the most serious financial crisis in World history, far surpassing that of 1929. It occurred immediately following “warnings” by the WHO that a covid-19 pandemic was imminent, thereby spearheading the fear campaign. There was  ample evidence of outright “conflict of interest” and fraud including foreknowledge, inside information, etc. which resulted in a massive concentration of money wealth by a handful of billionaires. That same day, millions of people Worldwide lost their lifelong savings. What was the justification for the WHO’s shock and awe statements. This imminent threat was based on 1078 (flawed) RT-PCR Covid positive cases outside China.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">3. Barely three weeks later, the <strong>March 11, 2020 lockdown</strong> with 44,279 cases Worldwide outside China were used to justify home confinement, social distancing and the closure of economic activity Worldwide leading to poverty and mass unemployment.</p>
<p>And then in early November 2020 it was the launching of the MRNA “Killer Vaccine” which has resulted in a trend of mortality and morbidity. See the latest figures below.</p>
<div>
<hr/>
<p>EU/EEA/Switzerland to 28 August 2021 – <strong>23,252 Covid-19 injection related deaths and 2,166,285 injuries, </strong>per EudraVigilance Database.</p>
<div>
<p>UK to 18 August 2021 – <strong>1,609 Covid-19 injection related deaths and 1,165,636 injuries</strong>, per MHRA Yellow Card Scheme.</p>
