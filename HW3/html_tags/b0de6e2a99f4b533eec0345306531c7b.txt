<p><strong>Global Research Note</strong></p>
<p><em>We bring to the attention of our readers this carefully documented study. </em></p>
<p><em>While the Chinese authorities announced <strong>TWO  YEARS AGO on January 7, 2020</strong> that they had isolated and identified “a new type of virus” no details were provided. Then on the 28th of January 2020, the US Centre for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) stated that <a href="https://www.cdc.gov/coronavirus/2019-ncov/lab/grows-virus-cell-culture.html">the novela corona virus had been isolated.</a>  </em></p>
<p><em>The central question raised in this study is the following: <strong>is there reliable evidence that SARS-CoV-2  has been isolated  from an “unadulterated sample taken from a diseased patient”?</strong></em></p>
<p><i>The study provides documentation based on Freedom of Information requests addressed to Health /Science institutions in a large number of countries. </i></p>
<p><em>The responses to these requests confirm that there is no record of isolation/ purification undertaken by the numerous Health /Science institutions which were contacted. </em></p>
<p><i>It is worth noting that according to the Berlin Virology Institute, the WHO in January 2020 did not have in its possession details regarding the isolation and identity of SARS-CoV-2. </i></p>
<p><i>Moreover, because the relevant details concerning isolation /purification were not available, the WHO decided pursuant to the <a href="https://www.eurosurveillance.org/content/10.2807/1560-7917.ES.2020.25.3.2000045">advice of the Berlin Virology Institute</a> to “customize” The Real Time Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (rRT-PCR) </i><i>using  the “similar” 2003 SARS virus (subsequently renamed SARS-1) as “a point of reference” for detecting genetic fragments of the 2019 SARS-CoV-2.  </i></p>
<p><i></i><i>Bear in mind, this totally flawed RT-PCR test is being used not only to detect V-the virus, it is now being used to ‘detect” the variants of SARS-CoV-2.</i></p>
<p>Supporting documents including the responses by Health /Science institutions, CDC, etc are provided in this study. Also more documents can be consulted by downloading the relevant pdf files compiled by the researchers.</p>
<p><strong>Michel Chossudovsky</strong>, Global Research, August 4, 2021, January 6, 2022</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><i>(</i><i>The <a href="https://www.who.int/health-topics/severe-acute-respiratory-syndrome#tab=tab_1">Severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS)</a> is “a viral respiratory disease caused by a SARS-associated coronavirus”,  first identified in China in February 2003).</i></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p>***</p>
<p><em>Update as of October 29, 2021: We now have<b><a href="https://www.globalresearch.ca/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Institution-list-for-website7.xls"> 127 institutions</a> in over 25 </b><b>countries</b> on record – all failed to provide or cite even 1 record describing purification of the alleged covid virus from any patient sample on the planet, by anyone.  All the documents are <a href="https://www.fluoridefreepeel.ca/fois-reveal-that-health-science-institutions-around-the-world-have-no-record-of-sars-cov-2-isolation-purification/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" shape="rect" target="_blank">publicly available</a>. </em></p>
<p>*</p>
<p><em>Would a sane person mix a patient sample (containing various sources of genetic material and never proven to contain any particular virus) with transfected monkey kidney cells, fetal bovine serum and toxic drugs, then claim that the resulting concoction is “SARS-COV-2 isolate” and ship it off internationally for use in critical research (including vaccine and test development)?</em></p>
<p><strong><em>Because that’s the sort of fraudulent monkey business that’s being passed off as “virus isolation” by research teams around the world.</em></strong></p>
