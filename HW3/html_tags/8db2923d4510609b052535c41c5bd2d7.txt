<p><img alt="" class="wp-image-28408 aligncenter tie-appear" height="369" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 641px) 100vw, 641px" src="https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1.68mil-contact_final2-300x173.png" srcset="https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1.68mil-contact_final2-300x173.png 300w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1.68mil-contact_final2-1024x591.png 1024w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1.68mil-contact_final2-768x443.png 768w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1.68mil-contact_final2.png 1499w" width="641"/></p>
<p>In the tests, 44 beagle puppies who were just 6-8 months old were repeatedly injected with or force-fed an experimental drug for weeks, and then killed and dissected.</p>
<p><img alt="" class="wp-image-28412 aligncenter tie-appear" height="306" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" src="https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Safety-evaluation_final2-300x143.png" srcset="https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Safety-evaluation_final2-300x143.png 300w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Safety-evaluation_final2-1024x489.png 1024w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Safety-evaluation_final2-768x367.png 768w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/Safety-evaluation_final2.png 1465w" width="640"/></p>
<p>The documents also show that the <strong>Fauci’s NIH division paid to have the puppies’ vocal cords cut out so they couldn’t bark in the lab while they were being abused.</strong></p>
<p><img alt="" class="wp-image-28599 aligncenter tie-appear" height="292" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 640px) 100vw, 640px" src="https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/cordectomy_final-300x137.png" srcset="https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/cordectomy_final-300x137.png 300w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/cordectomy_final-1024x467.png 1024w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/cordectomy_final-768x350.png 768w, https://blog.whitecoatwaste.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/cordectomy_final.png 1500w" width="640"/></p>
<p>To add insult to injury, these dog tests ARE COMPLETELY UNNECESSARY! The NIH stated that the dogs tests were meant to support a submission to the Food and Drug Administration (FDA), but the FDA has stated clearly as recently as <a href="https://wjla.com/features/i-team/animals-drug-testing-fda-labs-animals" rel="noopener" target="_blank"><strong>this summer</strong></a>: “The FDA does not mandate that human drugs be studied in dogs.”</p></blockquote>

<p>The cruelty against these animals — unlike the cruelty Fauci’s exhibited over the last 20 months to Americans — is hard to ignore. The imagery and wholly sadistic nature of the torture of beagle puppies has apparently crossed the partisan wall and united those on both sides to make sure this stops immediately.</p>
<p>After the revelations, two dozen members of Congress signed onto a letter, lead by Republican Representative Nancy Mace of South Carolina’s 1st congressional district, with a number of questions directed at Fauci.</p>
<p>“We write with grave concerns about reports of costly, cruel, and unnecessary taxpayer-funded experiments on dogs commissioned by National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases,” the lawmakers wrote.</p>
<p>Pointing to White Coat Waste and media reports, the members of the House wrote that “from October 2018 until February 2019, NIAID spent $1.68 million in taxpayer funds on drug tests involving 44 beagle puppies.</p>
<p>“The dogs were all between six and eight months old. The commissioned tests involved injecting and force-feeding the puppies an experimental drug for several weeks, before killing and dissecting them.</p>
<p>“Of particular concern is the fact that the invoice to NIAID included a line item for ‘cordectomy.’ As you are likely aware, a cordectomy, also known as ‘devocalization,’ involves slitting a dog’s vocal cords in order to prevent them from barking, howling, or crying,” the letter went on.</p>
<p>“This cruel procedure – which is opposed with rare exceptions by the American Veterinary Medical Association, the American Animal Hospital Association, and others – seems to have been performed so that experimenters would not have to listen to the pained cries of the beagle puppies. This is a reprehensible misuse of taxpayer funds,” the lawmakers said.</p>
<p>The lawmakers demanded to know how many of these tests have been performed on dogs and what the cost has been. They also demanded to know why they have taken place despite the FDA’s lack of requirement for dog tests.</p>
<p>Highlighting the bipartisan support for the inquiry into Fauci is the fact that Democrats and Republicans equally signed onto the letter.</p>
