<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal"><em>This year marks a seminal turning point in human history. For the first time since human civilization began, our species is being genetically modified. Vaccine manufacturers have now made it possible for the human genome to be permanently altered—and humanity’s relationship with nature forever changed—by means of an experimental pharmaceutical injection that is being falsely referred to as a “vaccine.” </em></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal"><em>In light of this defining event, I believe we must take a sober look at the motives and acts that are revamping humanity as we know it. Simultaneously, we must examine our increasingly destructive treatment of the natural world.<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></em></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal"><em>In order to investigate the many variables that are hastening the demise of humanity and sabotaging our unique role as stewards of the earth and its billions of plant and animal species, I have divided this study into four main parts, which will appear as separate articles:<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></em></p>
<p class="yiv6784611055MsoNormal"><strong>In Part I:</strong> <strong>The Microbiome and The Virome</strong>, we will discover that we are literally swimming in a vast sea of genomic information that was essential for life to begin and flourish on this precious earth and that is still trying to help all species survive. The matrix of organisms that make up the microbiome have built a viromic information stream that has allowed for adaptation and biodiversity to occur on the planet. And that very same viromic information stream is responsible for building the human species.</p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">In<span class="yiv6360023949Apple-converted-space"> </span><b>Part lI</b>: <strong>Our War Against Nature</strong>, we will explore how our own reckless behaviour is destroying the environment, thereby moving us toward the sixth mass extinction. By that I mean, I will be covering the<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>real </i>environmental catastrophe,<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>not</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>the billionaire-funded “global warming/climate change” hoax initiated by the Club of Rome and further promulgated by the World Economic Forum (WEF).<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">In<span class="yiv6360023949Apple-converted-space"> </span><b>Part III:</b><span class="yiv6360023949Apple-converted-space"> </span><strong>What Happened in 2020</strong>, we will examine how this<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>real<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></i>environmental devastation has contributed to the “pandemic” that was rolled out in 2020, that led to the mass experimental injections of unknown substances into human “subjects” in 2021, and that has no foreseeable end. (I put “pandemic” in quotes because of its fraudulent character. Indeed, it is more accurately and aptly described as a plandemic, a scamdemic, a pseudo pandemic or any other term indicating fakery.) <span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">In<span class="yiv6360023949Apple-converted-space"> </span><b>Part IV</b>:<strong> Our Response</strong>, we will analyze the irresponsible and irrational response of most people on the planet to this so-called pandemic.<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">Parts II, III and IV will be published later by Global Research.</p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal"><strong>The Microbiome  </strong></p>
<p class="yiv0513329547MsoNormal">The microbiome (derived from the Greek words<span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>micro</i>, meaning “small,” and<span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>biotikos</i>, meaning “pertaining to life”) is a massive ecosystem consisting of trillions of microorganisms. Incredibly, some 40,000 species of<span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span><a href="https://www.clinicaleducation.org/resources/abstracts/scientists-discover-in-excess-of-40000-of-bacteria-in-the-human-gut/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">bacteria</a>, 300,000 species of<span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span><a href="https://academic.oup.com/icb/article/58/6/1132/5026008" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">parasites</a>, 65,000 species of<span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span><a href="https://biologywise.com/protozoa-classification-characteristics" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">protozoa</a>, and between 3.5 million and 5 million species of<span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span><a href="https://bsapubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.3732/ajb.1000298" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">fungi</a><span class="yiv0513329547Apple-converted-space"> </span>inhabit the environment around us and live in or on the human body. This complex world of microorganisms continually secretes a sea of viruses, which serve as a communication network for the bacteria, parasites, protozoa, and fungi. And, as we will discover shortly, these viruses have always been here to help us, not hinder us. In other words, they are life-affirming, not death-inducing.</p>

<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">It is not that the viruses are<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>causing</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>a disease. Rather, it is that they are simply presenting the body with a new genetic adaptation option. The body’s innate immune system then determines how much of that new information it will absorb. If the cells are in dire need of repair—perhaps as a result of poor dietary choices, a sedentary lifestyle, or toxicity in the environment—the virus will create an inflammation event as the body goes through its regenerative process. This is usually accompanied by a fever, loss of appetite, and an elevated white blood cell count. Such an inflammatory event is what we commonly refer to as “the flu.”<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">What we derogatively call an inflammatory event—implying it is<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>bad</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>for the body—is actually a part of the body’s healing process. The inflammation is needed to create regeneration within the body. It is acting on behalf of the body, not against it. But if the body’s microbiome is replete instead of wanting, it will not need an update, and therefore no inflammation will take place.<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">In the case of Sub-Saharan Africa, the ecosystem is dying. The collapse of nutrient-rich soil systems, poor water hygiene, a lack of basic sanitation, a chronically undernourished population, and the complete elimination of traditional organic farming—overtaken by the oxymoronic Green Revolution, foisted on developing countries by industrial agriculture—have caused a large portion of that population to develop an imbalance between their innate immune system and the environment. The syndrome known as “AIDS” is an expression of that imbalance. The HIV virus, which was first discovered by French virologist Luc Montagnier, has been falsely accused of being the primary culprit responsible for AIDS—a form of guilt by association. In actual fact, the HIV virus is benign and is<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>not</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>trying to take over the mechanics of any cell.<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">The real root of the problem is that the innate immune system of the Sub-Saharan African people has been degraded by a lack of nutrition to such an extent that they are falling prey to a myriad of illnesses, which have been collectively grouped under the single title “AIDS.” However, instead of coming to terms with the reality of what the dire ecological disaster is causing, “scientists” are blaming the HIV virus as a cover to hide decades of government and corporate environmental and economic crimes.</p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">From the information covered thus far, we can rightly conclude that it is impossible for viruses or pathogens to create infectious disease pandemics and epidemics—for there is<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>no such thing</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>as an infectious disease in the traditional sense of the term—examples being “AIDS,” “Ebola,” and other unfounded “viral” pandemics. Yes, pharmaceutical propaganda has been pushing the infectious disease paradigm on world thought for centuries. But the belief that such diseases exist is no more than an outgrowth of Pasteur’s debunked germ theory. What we commonly refer to as an epidemic or a pandemic is simply the result of a degraded innate immune system showing up in a segment of the planet’s population. The reasons for this degradation can include chemical poisoning from herbicides, pesticides, or genetically modified foods, which we will look at in more detail below.<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span></p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">As we can see by the above description of the virome, it is no exaggeration to say that the virome is the language of all life on earth. We are literally swimming in a vast sea of genomic information that was essential for life to begin and flourish on this precious earth and that is still trying to help all species survive. The matrix of organisms that make up the microbiome have built a viromic information stream that has allowed for adaptation and biodiversity to occur on the planet. And that very same viromic information stream is responsible for building the human species.</p>
<p class="yiv6360023949x_MsoNormal">Thus, humans are not<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>separate</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>from the virome and the microbiome but are, rather,<span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span><i>integral</i><span class="yiv6360023949x_Apple-converted-space"> </span>to the virome and microbiome’s vast, complex ecosystem. Yet we have increasingly placed ourselves in direct opposition to the very living system of which we are an intrinsic part: nature.</p>
